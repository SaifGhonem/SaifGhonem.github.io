About

       
         

            I am a Data Analyst focused on turning data into clear and actionable decisions.
            I work with real datasets and approach every project by understanding the problem before touching the tools.
            My work emphasizes data quality validation thoughtful modeling and insight driven dashboards.
            I aim to deliver analysis that supports decision making not just visualization.          

                  

            I believe good analysis starts with the right question not the right tool.
